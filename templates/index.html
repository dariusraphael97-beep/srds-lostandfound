Simple Process

How It Works

Getting reunited with your belongings is simple.

1

Found Something?

Fill out our quick form and upload a photo. Takes under 2 minutes — you're helping a classmate out.

2

Staff Reviews It

A member of the SRDS team reviews every submission before it goes public, keeping things accurate and safe.

3

Search & Browse

Search by keyword, filter by category, or scroll through items. Bookmark ones you're keeping an eye on.

4

Claim & Collect

Spot your item? Submit a claim with your student ID. Staff will verify and arrange pickup — no fuss.
